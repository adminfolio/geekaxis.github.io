Razer Kaira Gaming Headset

by amosayomide05

Posted on 2021-09-24


Razer Kaira X wired gaming headset has been launched for PlayStation and Xbox gamers. Razer Kaira X headset is priced at $59.99 (roughly Rs. 4,400) in the United States. The headset is now available to support at Raxer Official Website.

The headset is made up of Triforce 50mm drivers and has a frequency response range of 20Hz to 20,000Hz and has an impedance of 32 Ohms.

The gaming headset supports PC, Nintendo Switch, PlayStation, Mac, and mobile devices. It has a cable length of 1.3 metres. The gaming headset comes in different colors: Black, Blue, Green, Red, and White.

The Raxer Kaira X wired gaming headset has a frequency microphone response of 10Hz to 10000Hz. It also has a 3.5mm audio cable jack for cross-platform compatibility.

According to the company, from 5tg of October, you can now buy additional Kaira X controller color from their official website (consisting of Shock Blue, Pulse Red, and Electric Volt). Best of all the headset weighs only approximately 283g.
